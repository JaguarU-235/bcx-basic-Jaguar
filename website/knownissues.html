rB+ known issues
Here are some things to consider when using rB+

Collisions:

Now working as intended.

16-bit graphics:

Conversion added - requires further testing.

Sprite lists:

Sprite (or raptor) lists currently require the first and last objects in each list to remain active. You may wish to creat small dummy sprites (objects) to mark the start and end of yours lists and keep them active but >y=255.

Particle function:

Particles are currently drawn and cleared directly to and from the particle buffer screen directly. When asking a lot of the particle system, this can result in a particle buffer displayed towards the top of the screen with missing or flickering particles as the particles only arrive after the lines have been processed by the OP. A triple buffer system will be introduced to allow drawing, clearing and displaying of particles in order to obtain a steady display. This will be exploitable for far more than simply displaying the particles :0)

Sound functions:

Some of the sound functions implemented appear to possibly control the MOD-side of the SE. We'll get around to researching this in time, but for now don't be surprised if some odd behaviour is observed away from simple MOD and sample playing and frequency setting.

Feel free to report any suspicious or inconsistent happenings

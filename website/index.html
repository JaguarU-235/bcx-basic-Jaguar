rB+ is GGN's BASIC for Atari Jaguar, placing much of the functionality of CJ's raptor into the hands of entry-level programmers.

It is built on Kevin Diggins' BCX - the BASIC to C translator. Combined, it offers the friendliness and ease of use of BASIC, the flexibility of C and the performance of raptor.

Download the rB+ installer - our recommended option giving the best functionality.

If installers are not your thing, then grab the package via: GitHub or Bitbucket.
